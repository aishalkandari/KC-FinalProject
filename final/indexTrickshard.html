OLLIE

The motion of an ollie is using one foot to snap the tail of the board downward then you have the board aiming up and sliding your front foot at the right time in order to level that board out in the air.

FRONTSIDE 180

When the skater is precisely on their board, the tail rotates toward the front side of their body.

VARIAL FLIP

Basically, it's a shove-it and kick flip all at once, what happens is you flick the board & position your foot and kicking it and giving it a little twist at the same time.

180 AERIAL

in this tricks you have to go up the ramp (to the top) & getting in the air & turning 180 deg, and coming back down the same side of the ramp .

HEEL FLIP

You put your back foot at the end of the board and your front foot about half of the board then pop up, kick and land.
